Cloudiness

It seems that there tend to be more clouds near the equator as well as towards the North and South Poles. There are less clouds in cities between 20 and 40 Deg. of latitude. This makes sense as there are a lot of rainforests near the equator, and cities near the north, such as Seattle, Washington, tend to experience more rainfall than in Southern California.
